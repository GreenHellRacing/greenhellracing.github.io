Green Hell Racing League

We provide a racing league for Xbox and PC players across EU and NA

Assetto Corsa Competizione

Our ACC league runs on a Saturday at 16:45 UTC each week. You can also find us on SimGrid and sign up for the races there.

F1 2021

Join our Discord Server to qualify and register for a team in our F1 championship. Races take place on a Thursday at 19:30 UTC weekly.

Other categories such as F2 may also be available to compete in soon!

Dirt Rally 2.0

Join our Dirt Rally 2.0 club to compete in our monthly league championships.
